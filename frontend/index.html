Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our services and platform.
How can I register as a guide?
To register as a guide, please contact our support team at teamwork17223@gmail.com with your credentials and relevant experience. Our team will review your application and guide you through the registration process.
How do I update my task progress?

To update your progress:

  1. Open your assigned task from the Dashboard
  2. Break it down into subtasks (if needed)
  3. Mark subtasks as complete as you finish them
  4. Save changes - your progress updates automatically
The system calculates your overall progress based on completed subtasks.
Can students join multiple teams?

Our system enforces a one-team policy for students:

  • Each student can only be active in one team at a time
  • To switch teams, you must first leave your current team
Can I assign multiple tasks to a team?

Yes, you can assign multiple tasks to any team. The system will:

  • Automatically distribute tasks to members
  • Track each member's workload
